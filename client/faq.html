Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our personal finance management app

What is Finance Manager?

Finance Manager is a personal finance management application designed to help you track your expenses, manage your income, and plan your budget effectively.

How do I sign up?

You can sign up by clicking the "Sign Up" button on the homepage or any of the pricing plans. Fill in your details and follow the instructions to create an account.

Is my data secure?

Yes, your data is secure with Finance Manager. We use advanced encryption methods and follow industry best practices to ensure your information is protected.

Can I link my bank accounts?

Yes, Finance Manager allows you to link your bank accounts to automatically track your transactions and manage your finances more efficiently.

What are the subscription plans available?

We offer three subscription plans: Basic, Premium, and Enterprise. Each plan provides different features and benefits to suit your needs. You can find more details on the Pricing page.
